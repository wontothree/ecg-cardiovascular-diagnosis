Development

At the InBody Makerthon 2023 organized by InBody, our team developed a personalized heath management solution using InBody data and ECG-based cardiovascular diagnosing device. As a result, we received the Excellence Award and a prize of 2 million KRW.

I applied a CNN model to denoise and normalize ECG sensor data, and integrated it with InBody data to develop a machine learning algorithm for cardiovascular disease diagnosis.
